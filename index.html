How It Works

01

Upload

Drop your PDFs, scanned images, or handwritten records. Batch upload supported.

02

Queue & Process

Documents enter a processing queue. Claude Vision reads each page using a preset extraction template.

03

Extract & Validate

Fields, tables, sections, and handwritten values are extracted. Low-confidence items are flagged for review.

04

Export

Download structured Excel spreadsheets with normalized data ready for your workflow.

Capabilities

What Gets Extracted

Structured Fields

Named key-value pairs extracted with confidence scores. Handwritten values are flagged.

Document Sections

Headers, paragraphs, certifications, and checkbox states — organized by page.

Tables & Intervals

Row/column data from interval tables, production schedules, and material logs.

Review Flags

Ambiguous handwriting, conflicting data, and low-confidence values flagged for review.
